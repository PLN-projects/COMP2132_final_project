About Dice Game!

Welcome to the dice game! This website uses Javascript to make a website that allows you to play a game of dice with a computer opponent. There are no prizes and you cannot bet any money since we don't support gambling but you can play as many new games as you like. Play to your hearts content!

How to play

Press the New Game button to start. After you press that button a form will appear to ask you your name, once you enter your name and press the submit button on the form the game will start. When the game starts, press the Roll Dice button to roll your dice. Every time you roll your dice, your CPU opponent will also roll their dice. You can press the New Game button at any time to start a new game.

Rules of Dice game

  • The game ends once the player rolls their dice 3 times.
  • Each time you press the roll dice button you will roll 2 dice.
  • If you roll a 1 on either of your dices, you gain zero points on your turn.
  • If your two dice are the exact same number, you gain double the points.
  • If you roll ones on both dice you do not gain double points. You will gain zero points.
  • When the game ends, the person with the highest score wins.
